# ForgeStore Marketing Onboarding

## Brand Overview

**Name**: ForgeStore
**Tagline**: Where the Workshop Meets the World
**Positioning**: AI-powered multi-vendor marketplace for independent sellers and buyers

## Core Features (Truthful)

### 1. AI Shopping Assistant
- 24/7 chat-based AI that helps customers find products
- Answers questions about product details, availability, pricing
- Compares products across vendors
- **Available to every customer** — not a premium feature

### 2. AI Product Recommendations
- Personalized suggestions based on browsing history
- Cart-based recommendations ("customers also bought")
- Popular items and trending products
- **Available to every customer** — not a premium feature

### 3. Multi-Vendor Marketplace
- Independent sellers with their own storefronts
- Vendor ratings and reviews
- Separate vendor fulfillment and shipping
- Compare products across multiple sellers

### 4. Secure Payments (Paystack Only)
- Card payments
- Bank transfers
- Mobile payments
- International payments supported

### 5. Vendor Tools
- Vendor dashboard with sales analytics
- Wallet system with automated payouts
- Ad campaign management
- Product inventory management
- Order fulfillment tracking

### 6. Affiliate System
- Customer-to-vendor referral links
- Vendor-to-vendor affiliate tracking
- Commission tracking and payouts
- Points and rewards system

## Target Audience

### Primary: Nigerian Online Shoppers
- Age: 18-45
- Tech-savvy, mobile-first
- Looking for variety and competitive pricing
- Value secure payments and reliable delivery

### Secondary: Independent Sellers
- Small to medium businesses
- Artisans and craftspeople
- Online resellers
- Looking for affordable e-commerce platform

## Competitive Advantages

1. **AI-First Approach**: Every customer gets AI assistant and recommendations
2. **Multi-Vendor**: Compare products across sellers
3. **Transparent Pricing**: No hidden fees, clear commission structure
4. **Secure Payments**: Paystack integration with buyer protection
5. **Vendor-Friendly**: Low barrier to entry, automated payouts

## Content Guidelines

### Do
- Highlight AI features as core differentiator
- Emphasize multi-vendor marketplace benefits
- Show real vendor success stories (when available)
- Use accurate statistics and metrics

### Don't
- Claim false numbers (e.g., "500+ vendors" when we have fewer)
- Promise features that don't exist
- Compare to competitors without verification
- Use misleading testimonials
