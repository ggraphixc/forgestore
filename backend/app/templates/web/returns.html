Returns Policy

Not satisfied? Return your order hassle-free within 30 days.

Return Eligibility

Eligible for Return

  • +Items within 30 days of delivery
  • +Unused and in original condition
  • +Original packaging intact
  • +Defective or damaged items
  • +Wrong item received

Non-Returnable Items

  • -Personalized or custom-made items
  • -Perishable goods (food, flowers)
  • -Intimate or sanitary products
  • -Digital downloads or gift cards
  • -Items marked as "Final Sale"

How to Return an Item

1

Initiate Your Return

Log in to your account, go to My Orders, and click Request Return on the relevant order. Select the items and reason for return.

2

Get Your Return Label

Once approved, you'll receive a prepaid return shipping label via email. Print it and attach it to your package.

3

Pack & Ship

Securely pack the item in its original packaging. Drop off at any authorized pickup point or schedule a free pickup.

4

Get Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, your refund will be processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method.
