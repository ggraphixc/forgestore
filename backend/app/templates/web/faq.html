Browse our marketplace, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay via Paystack, bank transfer, or other supported payment methods. You'll receive an order confirmation via email immediately after placing your order.

You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it, as long as it hasn't been shipped. Go to My Orders and select the order you want to change. After shipment, you'll need to wait for delivery and then initiate a return.

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery takes 1–2 business days. Same-day delivery is available in Lagos for orders placed before 12 PM. International shipping typically takes 7–14 business days.

Once we receive your return, it takes 1–2 days for inspection and 3–5 business days to process the refund to your original payment method. Bank transfers may take an additional 1–2 business days to appear.
